ABOUT ME

CHRISTOPHER BAILEY

Born in 1992, with an intense love for creativity and music, I like to see the world through more than just my eyes but experience it with my soul. I put my all into my passions and I don't bother wasting time on things i have no desire for. My true desire in life is to be able to spread love through creativity and make a living while doing so. I was taught growing up to never settle for a job you hate but instead work your hardest doing something you love and I intend to do just that!
